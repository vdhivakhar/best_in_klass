Our Story

Simple food, authentic soul

Best In Klass has been serving the IT community of Whitefield with honest, wholesome South Indian vegetarian food. Located inside Sigma Soft Tech Park, we are the go-to spot for a satisfying breakfast, quick lunch, or a relaxed dinner after a long day.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional recipes — crispy dosas, fluffy idlis, comforting upma, and much more. Simple. Clean. Delicious.
